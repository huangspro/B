Inclusion criteria: Met diagnostic criteria for schizophrenia (Chinese criteria), not received antipsychotics for > 3 months or discontinued, PANSS ≥ 60.

Exclusion criteria: Endocrine disease, malnutrition, diabetes, or other mental disorders, allergic to one of three drugs, refusal to participate.

Pretreatment: No statistically significant differences.
